# Easiest Dinner Ever

## Servings: 4

## Time to prepare: 15 minutes

## Ingredients: Pasta sauce, spaghetti, parmesan cheese


## Equipment needed: pot


## Steps for preparation:

1. Boil water in a pan
2. Add spaghetti and boil for 6-8 minutes
3. Drain water from pan
4. Add sauce
5. Top with cheese
